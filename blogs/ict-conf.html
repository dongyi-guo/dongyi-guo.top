TasICT Tasmanian ICT Conference & Gala Dinner

I got this precious volunteering opportunity from Dr. Ming Wong at University of Tasmania and had the honour working directly with Mr. Steve Adermann, the CEO of TasICT himself. I had the honour to greet lots of precious guests, hosts, and especially her ladyship, Hon Madeleine Ogilvie MP. I also attended the Gala Dinner after the conference, spotted on massive highlights and insights of the ICT industry in Tasmania, and got to know lots of amazing and beloved guests and industry professionals.
